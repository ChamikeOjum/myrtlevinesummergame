About Us

Myrtaceae High School Onitsha is dedicated to excellence. We raise God-fearing leaders and provide a conducive environment for learning and growth.

Mission: To provide a conducive environment, effective and focused teachers, and quality teaching to achieve excellent results.

Vision: To be a world-class school developing children's skills, knowledge, and character to their full potential.

Academics

We offer a blend of the Montessori Method and the British Early Years Foundation Stage Curriculum, ensuring effective learning outcomes.

Preschool and Nursery

Exceptional child care promoting wholesome development in young children.

Primary

Opportunities to learn, think, and reason, with a focus on individual growth patterns.

Secondary

Emphasis on discipline and total training of the child in spirit, soul, and body.

Admissions

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds to join our institution. Our admission process is designed to be smooth and efficient.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records
  • Two passport photographs

How to Apply

Fill out the application form available on our website or visit our admissions office for assistance. We look forward to welcoming your child to Myrtaceae High School Onitsha.
